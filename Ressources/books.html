Welcome to the Book section! Here, you'll find a collection of books covering various topics in physics, from classical mechanics to quantum field theory and beyond.

Classical Mechanics

  • Harris Benson, Mathieu Lachance, Marc Séguin - Physique 1 - Mécanique
    Introduction complète à la mécanique classique pour étudiants de premier cycle.

  • Harris Benson, Mathieu Lachance, Marc Séguin - Physique 1 - Solutionnaire: Mécanique
    Recueil détaillé de solutions.

  • Pierre Gaspard - Mécanique Analytique
    Ancien syllabus de Gaspard.

Electromagnetism

  • Harris Benson, Mathieu Lachance, Marc Séguin - Physique 2 - Electricité Magnétisme
    Manuel de base couvrant les principes fondamentaux de l'électricité et du magnétisme.

  • Harris Benson, Mathieu Lachance, Marc Séguin - Physique 2 - Solutionnaire: Electricité Magnétisme
    Recueil détaillé de solutions.

Statistical Mechanics

  • Pierre Gaspard, Bortolo Mognetti - Physique statistique
    Syllabus de cours de physique statistique.

Wave Physics & Optics

  • Harris Benson, Mathieu Lachance, Marc Séguin - Physique 3 - Ondes, optique et physique moderne
    Manuel combinant les ondes, l'optique et une introduction à la physique moderne.

  • Harris Benson, Mathieu Lachance, Marc Séguin - Physique 3 - Solutionnaire: Ondes, optique et physique moderne
    Recueil détaillé de solutions.

Quantum Mechanics

  • Claude Cohen-Tannoudji, Bernard Diu - Mécanique quantique. Tome I
    Introduction aux concepts fondamentaux et formalismes de la mécanique quantique.

  • Claude Cohen-Tannoudji, Bernard Diu - Mécanique quantique. Tome II
    Applications avancées et approfondissement des méthodes mathématiques.

  • Claude Cohen-Tannoudji, Bernard Diu - Mécanique quantique. Tome III
    Étude des particules (fermions et bosons), corrélations quantiques et intrication.

  • Pierre Gaspard - Mécanique quantique
    Ancien syllabus de Gaspard.

Quantum Field Theory

  • Michael E. Peskin and Daniel V. Schroeder - An Introduction To Quantum Field Theory
    A standard textbook for beginners, focusing on Feynman diagrams, perturbation theory, and the basics of the Standard Model.

  • Steven Weinberg - The Quantum Theory of Fields: Volume I Foundations
    A rigorous, advanced treatment of quantum field theory with a strong emphasis on symmetry and foundational principles.

  • Jean Zinn-Justin - Quantum Field Theory and Critical Phenomena
    Combines quantum field theory with statistical physics, focusing on critical phenomena and interdisciplinary applications.

  • Matthew D. Schwartz - Quantum Field Theory and the Standard Model
    Provides a modern introduction to quantum field theory with an emphasis on practical calculations and the Standard Model.

  • Reinhold A. Bertlmann - Anomalies in Quantum Field Theory
    A detailed exploration of anomalies in quantum field theory.

Relativity

  • Christiane Schomblond et Glenn Barnich - Introduction à la Relativité Générale
    Présente les principes fondamentaux de la relativité générale avec une approche pédagogique.

  • Matthias Blau - Lecture Notes on General Relativity
    Comprehensive lecture notes covering both basic and advanced topics in general relativity, suitable for self-study.

  • Tohru Eguchi, Peter B. Gilkey, Andrew J.. Hanson - Gravitation, Gauge Theories and Differential Geometry
    Explores the connections between gravitation, gauge theories, and differential geometry, offering a mathematical perspective.

  • Charles W. Misner, Kip S. Thorne, Wheeler - Gravitation (Parts A-G)
    The bible of general relativity in multiple parts.

  • Piotr T. Chruściel - Geometry of Black Holes
    Great for studying black holes. See also his YouTube videos.

Cosmology

  • Daniel Baumann - Cosmology
    A graduate-level introduction, suitable for students familiar with general relativity.

  • Viatcheslav Mukhanov - Physical Foundations of Cosmology
    Advanced text for students and researchers, delving into theoretical cosmology.

  • Scott Dodelson, Fabian Schmidt - Modern Cosmology
    For graduate students, covering both theoretical frameworks and observational data.

  • Edward W. Kolb, Michael S. Turner - The Early Universe
    An advanced exploration for researchers into the physics of the universe's beginnings.

Beyond Standard Model and Quantum Gravity

  • Carlo Rovelli, Francesca Vidotto - Covariant Loop Quantum Gravity: An Elementary Introduction to Quantum Gravity and Spinfoam Theory
    An introductory book for advanced students interested in loop quantum gravity and spinfoam models.

Mathematics

  • Abramowitz, Milton, Stegun, Irena A. - Handbook of Mathematical Functions with Formulas, Graphs, and Mathematical Tables
    A classic reference for mathematical functions, widely used for its extensive formulas, graphs, and numerical tables.

Miscellaneous

  • David Wright - Mathematics and Music
    Explores the connections between mathematics and music, including harmony, symmetry, and Fourier analysis.
